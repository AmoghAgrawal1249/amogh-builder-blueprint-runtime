# Acme Platform Rollout Notes

Client: Acme Manufacturing
Opportunity: Platform rollout pitch
Date: June 18, 2026
Owner: Sarah Chen, Account owner

## Summary

Acme is interested in moving forward with the platform rollout, but the main implementation concern is whether the migration can fit inside the Q3 timeline. The executive sponsor asked for a clearer rollout readiness plan before the final decision.

## Stakeholder Context

The VP of Operations is the most active stakeholder. The CIO is involved in the decision, but the VP of Operations is driving the day-to-day evaluation and wants confidence that the internal team can support the schedule.

## Decision Feedback

The buying committee responded positively to the revised implementation plan. Their remaining decision criteria are:

- confidence in rollout readiness
- timeline risk mitigation
- executive reporting during migration
- support coverage during the first month after go-live

## Pricing and Procurement

Procurement asked whether the rollout support package changes the commercial proposal. No discount was requested yet, but pricing may become relevant if the implementation timeline expands.

## Recommended Handoff Context

Mention implementation timeline as the primary active concern. The safest handoff should say that Acme wants validation of rollout readiness before final approval, rather than claiming that pricing is the main blocker.
