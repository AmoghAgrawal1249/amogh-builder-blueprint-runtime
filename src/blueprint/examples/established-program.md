## Candidate Example Set

### Copilot Readiness

Signal: client is planning Copilot rollout and handles sensitive data.

Connect: AI governance/data security consultant.

Reason: permissions, data exposure, adoption plan, and acceptable-use rules should be checked before rollout.

### Healthcare EHR Migration

Signal: healthcare client is planning EHR migration with unclear access and backup/recovery discussion.

Connect: healthcare GRC/BCDR consultant.

Reason: patient data, operational continuity, and HIPAA-sensitive workflow create a specific need.

### Acquisition Trigger

Signal: client announced acquisition.

Connect: M&A cyber/integration consultant.

Reason: identity, endpoint, vendor, network, and compliance integration risks may surface before systems merge.

### Vendor Gap With Evidence

Signal: marketplace flags email-security gap and client has repeated phishing tickets.

Connect: email security/IAM or partner owner.

Reason: vendor signal becomes credible only because client evidence supports it.

## Draft Example Shape

```text
Ahead of [client moment], [consultant/practice] may be worth a quick internal check. The trigger is [specific signal], and the client-specific reason is [risk/timing/context].

Suggested ask: have [consultant/practice] give you a short view before [meeting/review/proposal]. No client intro unless you think the angle is useful.
```

## Tone

High-signal, precise, and action-oriented. These examples should feel like they came from someone who understands the account.
